ONE STOP SOLUTION

Frameway Industries have strong foundations in a One-Stop solution for various projects and industries in die making, transfer, progressive, single hit & hydraulic sheet metal stamping.

While renown in the industry for high precision micro motor cases and fine automotive parts, Frameway would gladly assist other industries.

OVER 25 YEARS OF PRECISION ENGINEERING

Frameway strives to be an engineering leader in all transfer/progressive/hydraulic press stamping. With a large historical know how and being in Hong Kong, Frameway will gladly assist you in developing your projects.
